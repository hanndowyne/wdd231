Anime Romance Series Website Plan

Project planning document for the upcoming anime POV romance website.

Site Name

Romance stories

This name reflects the anime POV romance series I am creating and fits the theme of immersive storytelling.

Site Purpose

The website will introduce my anime romance series, showcase characters, share updates about new episodes, and provide a place for fans to follow the project.

Scenarios

  • “Where can I watch the next episode of this anime romance ?”
  • “Who are the main characters, and what are their personalities ?”

Color Scheme

Using a simple sunset palette:

  • Coral Sunset used for buttons, headings and key highlights.
  • Soft Peach used for backgrounds and soft accents.

Typography

  • Montserrat used for headings
  • Open Sans used for body text and paragraphs
